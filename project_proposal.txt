Executive Summary
DaffoTrack AI is an intelligent, Android-based academic companion designed to address the lack of personalized academic guidance at Daffodil International University (DIU). With a large student body, consistent one-on-one faculty counseling is challenging, often leading to suboptimal academic planning. Our proposed solution is a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) that leverages Artificial Intelligence to track student performance, predict future grades, and provide a real-time chat advisor for administrative queries. This project aims to significantly improve student CGPA trajectories while reducing the administrative burden on faculty members. We are requesting a minimal budget of BDT 8,000 to cover secure cloud hosting and premium AI API processing during the MVP phase.

Problem Statement / Needs Assessment
At Daffodil International University, the student-to-advisor ratio makes it virtually impossible for faculty members to provide continuous, personalized academic roadmaps for every individual. Students frequently struggle to calculate the necessary marks required in upcoming finals to achieve their target CGPA. Furthermore, administrative friction exists where students waste valuable time seeking answers to standard policy questions (e.g., prerequisite course requirements, retake/improvement policies, or mid-term absence rules). Without data-driven insights and instant support, many students make uninformed course selections, leading to academic burnout and lower overall graduation grades.
Project Goals and Objectives (SMART)
* Specific: Develop an Android-based AI MVP capable of analyzing course data and answering DIU-specific administrative queries.
* Measurable: Achieve a 90% accuracy rate in AI responses to standard university policy questions during the beta testing phase.
* Achievable: Utilize the team's existing skills in Java, PHP, and LLM API integration to build the system within 45 days.
* Relevant: Directly aligns with DIU's initiative to bridge the AI implementation gap in academic administration.
* Time-bound: Deliver a fully functional MVP for final assessment by June 10, 2026.

	Risk Analysis and Mitigation Plan
1. Risk: AI Hallucinations (Providing incorrect university rules).
   * Mitigation: We will implement strict prompt engineering and context-binding in the backend API to restrict the AI to authorized DIU data only.
2. Risk: Data Privacy Concerns.
   * Mitigation: The mobile app will not communicate directly with the AI. All requests will pass through our secure PHP proxy, which will strip personally identifiable information (PII) before sending data to the LLM.
3. Risk: API Downtime during Presentation.
   * Mitigation: We will implement a local caching system and fallback default responses within the app to ensure it remains stable during the live demonstration.
